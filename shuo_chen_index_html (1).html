About

Scientific curiosity with a human-centered lens.

My interest in biology began with a concern for public health and a broader question: how can science improve health in a way that is both meaningful and widely accessible? Over time, that question expanded into an interest in biotechnology, biomedical engineering, and the role of intelligent systems in accelerating research.

I am especially drawn to work that combines rigor with imagination. Whether in research, music, or visual storytelling, I care about precision, depth, and the possibility of building something that lasts.
